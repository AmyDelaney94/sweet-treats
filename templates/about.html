About Us

Sweet Treats is a place for bakers of all levels, that allows those with a love of baking and all things sweet to share and discover new and exciting recipes. Sweet Treats is a place to learn, share, and be inspired to try new things. The aim of this site is to be the home of everyones favourite recipes.

By logging into Sweet Treats you can take advantage of user benefits such as creating and sharing your own recipes with others, commenting and liking posts by other members and finding new bakes that you can try at home!

Remember; Good things come to those who bake! Take it easy and enjoy the process

Top Tips:
  • To navigate this site please use the home button or click to go back to the home page where you can explore a variety of delicious recipes!
  • When creating your delicious recipes please use the bullet point or numbered list options when filling in the Ingredients and Instructions sections to ensure your post is displayed correctly.
  • When creating a recipe, please ensure you select your own name as the author from the dropdown menu. This will guarantee that you can make changes later on.
  • To get the most out of this site like your favourites so you can easily find them later!
