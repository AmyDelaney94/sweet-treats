About Us

Sweet Treats is a platform that allows those with a love of baking and all things sweet to share and discover new and exciting recipes.

Top Tips:
  • To navigate this site please use the Recipe button or click to go back to the home page full of delicious recipes for you to explore!
  • When creating your delicious recipes please use the bullet point or numbered list options when filling in the Ingredients and Instructions sections to ensure your post is displayed correctly.

By logging into Sweet Treats you can take advantage of user benefits such creating and sharing your own recipes with others, commenting and liking posts by other memebers and finding new bakes that you can try at home!
